Essential Safety Tips

1

Use Strong Passwords

Create unique passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Use a password manager and enable two-factor authentication.

2

Think Before You Click

Verify links before clicking. Hover over URLs to see the actual destination and be wary of shortened links from unknown sources.

3

Keep Software Updated

Install security updates promptly. Updates often include patches for vulnerabilities that criminals exploit.

4

Protect Personal Information

Limit what you share online. Adjust privacy settings on social media and never share sensitive data publicly.

5

Use Secure Networks

Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions. Use a VPN when connecting to untrusted networks.

6

Report Suspicious Activity

If something seems wrong, report it immediately. Contact your school's IT department or local authorities.
